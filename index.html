What is Binary Options?

Binary options are financial instruments that allow you to predict if the price of an asset will rise or fall within a specific time frame.

With binary options on Quotex:

  • Choose an asset (forex, crypto, commodities)
  • Predict UP (CALL) or DOWN (PUT)
  • Set expiry time (as short as 1 minute)
  • If your prediction is correct, you earn a profit
  • If wrong, you lose your investment
